# Code of Conduct

[Українська версія](docs/uk/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers of **SelenaCore** pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are building a **private, offline, self-hosted smart home** — privacy, security, and respect are core values of this project.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Maintaining high standards of security and privacy

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information (including IP addresses, device tokens, or personal data)
- Any behavior that could compromise the security or privacy of the system
- Spamming or off-topic discussions

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.
They have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer:

Open an issue at [github.com/dotradepro/SelenaCore/issues](https://github.com/dotradepro/SelenaCore/issues) or contact [@dotradepro](https://github.com/dotradepro) on GitHub.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.
