Step 1: Scan for satellites

Power on the ESP32 satellite and hold the pairing button for 3 seconds until the LED blinks blue.

Step 2: Choose a device

Tap the satellite you want to add.

Step 3: Wi-Fi credentials

The satellite will join this network and connect back to the hub.

Step 4: Name & location

Give the satellite a name and assign it to a room.
